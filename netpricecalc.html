What is a Net Price Calculator?
A Net Price Calculator is an algorithm that Universities use to determine the overall cost of attendance for a student at a university. This overall cost of attendance typically includes tuition, food, and accomodation, and is based upon a number of factors that are available on the university sites.
How does CollAssist's Tool work?
This tool allows you to input universities, and access their net price calculators from our database. Users must enter the full name of the university whose NPC they wish to access.
