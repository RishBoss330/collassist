here are some popular and strong extra-curriculars that can assist your college chances, relating to your major. Note that other ECs, such as sports, the arts and others can greatly boost your chances too!
Engineering Major
  • Building a contraption
  • An Internship
  • Starting a YT/Podcast or authoring a research paper
Medicine
  • Shadowing at a clinic/hospital
  • Volunteering and Caring
  • Partaking in Research Activities/Groups
Economics and Finance
  • Internships
  • Forage Simulations
  • Running a small business, the greater revenue the better!
Law
  • Debate societies
  • Model United Nations
  • Leadership at clubs, possibly with speaking/argumentative roles
Computer Science
  • A large-scale website/app
  • Courses and certifications
  • Starting large-scale coding tutorial classes
Philosophy
  • Debate, MUNs, Mock Trial
  • Summer Research Programmes
  • Authoring/Book Clubs
